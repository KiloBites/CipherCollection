Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod XOR CM

On the Subject of the XOR CM

A machine that knows no bounds when it comes to ciphers.

Take the Nth letter of the encrypted word. Convert it into its alphabetic position using 5-digit binary (A = 00001, B = 00010, ... Z = 11010).

Do the same with the Nth letter of the provided keyword.

Calculate the bitwise XOR of the two numbers as follows:

  • If two binary digits in the same position are equal, the resulting binary digit is 0.
  • Otherwise, it is 1.

Convert each number back into a new letter.

The resulting letters is your decrypted word.

Example

Encrypted word: GGVPOH

Keyword: UNBEND

G (00111) xor U (10101) = R (10010)
G (00111) xor N (01110) = I (01001)
V (10110) xor B (00010) = T (10100)
P (10000) xor E (00101) = U (10101)
O (01111) xor N (01110) = A (00001)
H (01000) xor D (00100) = L (01100)

Decrypted Word: RITUAL
