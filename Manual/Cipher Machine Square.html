On the Subject of the Square CM

A machine that knows no bounds when it comes to ciphers.

From the Shape Cipher module by TracksJosh.

Create a key using the keyword on screen 1 and the Boolean Expression on screen A. Make sure to replace any Js with Is. Rearrange the key into a 5x5 matrix.

Split the encrypted word into pairs of letters. If there is an odd amount of letters, leave the last letter as the odd letter. Depending on which instruction is required, apply it to each pair of letters.

If the Submit Button is white, follow the instructions under Encrypt Instructions. Otherwise follow the instructions under Decrypt Instructions.

Encrypt Instructions

  • If the 2 letters are the same, the encrypted pair is two of the letter diametrically opposite in the matrix.
  • Otherwise, start at the 2nd letter of the pair and determine the path to get to the 1st letter. Use the same directions from the 1st letter to get your 1st decrypted pair.
  • Then start at the 1st letter of the pair and determine the path to get to the 2nd letter. Use the same directions from the 2nd letter to get your 2nd decrypted pair.

Decrypt Instructions

  • If the 2 letters are the same, the encrypted pair is two of the letter diametrically opposite in the matrix.
  • Otherwise, start at the 1st letter of the pair and determine the path to get to the 2nd letter. Use the same directions from the 2nd letter to get your 1st decrypted pair.
  • Then start at the 2nd letter of the pair and determine the path to get to the 1st letter. Use the same directions from the 1st letter to get your 2nd decrypted pair.

Finally, if any of the letters on screen 2 match at the same position as any of the letters of your encrypted word, replace that letter with a J. You should now have an decrypted word.
